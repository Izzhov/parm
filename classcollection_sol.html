From Allen and Tildesley, p.

263: r(t + dt) = r(t) + c1 dt v(t) + c2 dt^2 a(t) + dr_G v(t + dt) = c0 v(t) + c1 - c2) dt a(t) + c2 dt a(t + dt) + dv_G

where c0 = exp(- h dt) c1 = (h dt)^-1 (1 - c0) c2 = (h dt)^-1 (1 - c1) There are expansions for all 3

dr_G and dv_G are drawn from correlated Gaussian distributions, with

sigma_r^2 = (h dt)^-1 (2 - (h dt)^-1 (3 - 4 exp(-h dt) + exp(-2 h dt)) sigma_v^2 = 1 - exp(-2 h dt) c_{rv} = (h dt)^-1 (1 - exp(-h dt))^2 / sigma_r / sigma_v

Those are the unitless versions, multiply by dt^2 kB T/m, kB T/m, and dt kB T/m respectively to get the unit-full versions in the book
